    ABOUT CSU

    Vedavyas Campus of Central Sanskrit University, New Delhi is situated in Himachal Pradesh. Himachal Pradesh is a land of pristine natural beauty, pure and perfect, that's why it is called Devbhumi. Politically and geographically, the land faced many upheavels. As a political entity, it came on existence on 15th April 1948, as a Union Territory of India. It was constitited by integrating many similar princely states known as Punjab Hill States. In 1954, some more parts were added, further after reorganization of Punjab in 1966 few more parts were annexed to it. Finally HP was given statehood on 25th January, 1971 with Shimla as its capital.
